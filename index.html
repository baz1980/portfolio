About Me

After completing highschool, i started a traineeship in telecommunications. subsequently spending 15 years within this industry performing many roles. i started as a data cabler, moving on to optic fibre splicing in both private and carrier networks. this has lead me work on many industry first technologies. namely, Hobart's first fibre to the home project in Newtown. Later in my telecommunications career, i worked as an applications engineer for Corning Cable Systems, teaching tradespeople how to properly use Corning's optic fibre, carrier networks products. i ended by working as a project manager, overseeing all aspects of carrier network delivery. from cable hauling and splicing, to cutovers/integration and directional drilling. in more recent years i have been operating my own business as an arborist.
